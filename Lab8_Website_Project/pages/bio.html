My Life

I am Gregory Fofran. I come from a mixed parentage of New Ireland, Madang, West New Britain, and East Sepik Provinces. I am the third born in a family of 2 beautiful girls and 3 strong boys, totaling 5. I was born at Kavieng General Hospital in 2002. When I turned 1, my family and I moved to West New Britain where I spent the next 4 years of childhood. In 2005, we moved to Madang, where all stages of my life have unfolded until today. In 2012, I enrolled in grade 3 at Erima Primary School in Raikos District of Madang Province, skipping elementary. In 2018, I was selected for Raikos Secondary School for high school and completed grade 10 in 2019. In 2020, I was selected to do grade 11 at Passam National School of Excellence in East Sepik Province and completed grade 12 in 2021. In 2022, I enrolled in the Bachelor of Information Technology program at Don Bosco Technological Institute in Port Moresby. Unfortunately, I couldn't continue in 2023 due to financial constraints and stayed home for two years. In October 2024, I applied to Divine Word University as a non-school leaver to take up Bachelor of Information Systems, and this is where I am now in my first year.
